Pizza

Beautiful NY pizza

What is to really say? It is perfection in a bite

Ingredients

  • flour
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • cheese
  • tomatoes
  • water
  • basil

Steps

  1. Use the water and flour to make dough. Dont forget to add salt
  2. Knead the dough to create gluten and let it sit in a fridge overnight
  3. Flatten out the dough and generously apply marinara sauce and then cheese
  4. Let it cook in the wood fire oven and keep checking on it to make sure it is crispy
  5. Apply basil and parm
