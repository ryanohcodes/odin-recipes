Sushi

A beautifully prepared nigiri sushi

Prepare sushi rice and have a slab of fish ready. Grab a half fist full of rice and compress that bad boy. Don't forget to put a dab of wasabi underneath the fish before compressing it.

Ingredients

  • rice
  • water
  • sushi vinegar

Steps

  1. Prepare Rice
  2. Prepare fish
  3. Scoop rice with vinegar hands
  4. Add a drop of wasabi and compress fish on top
