Fried Chicken

Description

Howling Ray's. Best fried chicken in LA

Ingredients

  • Chicken without bones
  • flour
  • Buttermilk
  • Oil
  • Special Spice Mix

Steps

  1. Get someone to prepare your chicken
  2. Let your chicken marinate in buttermilk for a day
  3. Coat it in flour and deep fry that bad boy
  4. Generously apply that spice mix
